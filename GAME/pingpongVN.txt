------------------------------------------Huong dan-----------------------------------------------
                                       1. Gioi thieu
Tro choi nay duoc lay y tuong tu mon the thao pingpong( hay con goi la bong ban). 
Voi luat choi don gian va cach dieu khien toi gian tro choi thuc su de tiep can cho moi doi tuong.


                              2. Huong dan su dung menu cai dat
Trong phan cai dat co 2 muc la "Ngon ngu" va "Doi phim bam".
Phan "Ngon ngu" giup nguoi choi chuyen doi giua hai ngon ngu Anh-Viet.
Phan "Doi phim bam" cho phep nguoi choi cai dat lai cac phim di chuyen theo y minh.


                                    3. Huong dan choi game
-Dieu khien:
Su dung cac phim co ban hoac cac phim ma ban da cai dat lai de di chuyen thanh do.
Nguoi choi 1 phim di chuyen len (w), phim di chuyen xuong (s).
Nguoi choi 2 phim di chuyen len (i), phim di chuyen xuong (k).

-Luat choi:
Ban dau bong se nam o tam san dau con hai thanh do lan luot nam o trung tam 2 ben bien.
Khi mot ben bat dau di chuyen thanh do bong se di chuyen ngau nhien ve hai phia trai phai.
Nguoi choi dung thanh do cua minh de ngan cho bong khong qua khoi bien gioi cua minh.
Moi lan bong roi khoi bien gioi mot ben thi ben con lai se duoc nhan diem.
Co 2 moc quan trong can chu y de phan dinh thang thua:
+Khi co mot nguoi choi dat 4 diem thi vong dau tiep theo se la vong quyet dinh (match points).
Neu ho ghi duoc them 1 diem nua thi tro choi se ket thuc va ho se la nguoi chien thang.
+Trong truong hop ca hai nguoi choi deu dat duoc 4 diem ta se buoc vao vong dau phu (overtime).
Trong vong nay nguoi choi can dat duoc hai diem lien tuc de thang.
